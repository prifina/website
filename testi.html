Fully Private

Your information and data remain privately stored behind a secure, end-to-end encrypted connection. You hold all the encryption keys and maintain full control over your personal data. No one else—not us, or any other party—can access your private data, message logs, or data profiles unless you explicitly authorize them. It stays truly yours as long as you store your own memories.

Your Ownership

iPrifina processes you AI in five containerized core services that learn to map thoughts to actions in your own private memory vault - think Google neurons growing new pathways. Your AI only takes pages into its carefully planned infrastructure and learns from them just like any other brain-like apps, then returns your data base raw, allowing you to try out new datasets just like you'd normally do.
